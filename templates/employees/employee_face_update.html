Tips for Best Face Recognition
DO:
  • Use a clear, high-quality image (minimum 400x400px)
  • Ensure the face is front-facing and centered
  • Use good, even lighting (avoid harsh shadows)
  • Maintain a neutral expression
  • Ensure the entire face is visible
  • Use recent photos
DON'T:
  • Wear sunglasses or face masks
  • Use blurry or low-resolution images
  • Include multiple people in the photo
  • Use extreme angles or side profiles
  • Have objects obscuring the face
  • Use images with heavy filters or editing
